About Us

We, “RAF Industrial Products & Services”, have established a noteworthy position in the domain of trading and supplying Fasteners. Our long-lasting relationship with credible market vendors allow us to offer the customers with superior quality fasteners.

In addition, these are highly demanded in numerous industries for their fine finish, high tensile strength, high grip powers and corrosion resistance. Customers can avail these products from us in varied dimensions and sizes at affordable prices.

Our procured products are safely stored at the spacious warehouse till their final delivery to customers. Customized packaging is also offered by us so as to meet the requirements of clients. In addition, optimum safety is ensured during the transit process of the products.

We are a renowned supplier and trader of a distinct array of Fasteners

Our range has gained huge acclamation in the industry for high tensile strength, dimensional accuracy, fine finish and durability.
